Description

Temporary web site for the MEASURE magnetometer array. MEASURE is an acronym for Magnetometers along the Eastern Atlantic Seaboard for Undergraduate Research and Education. Measure is a meridional array of paired magnetometers along the east coast of the United States. Begun in 1998. The magnetometers were constructed by the UCLA magnetometer group.
