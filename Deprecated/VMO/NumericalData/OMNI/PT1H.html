OMNI Hourly Data Set

ResourceID
spase://VMO/NumericalData/OMNI/PT1H

Description

Hourly averaged, multi-source, near-Earth solar wind magnetic
field, plasma and energetic proton flux data, plus AE, AU, AL, Kp, ap, and Dst
geomagnetic indices, Polar Cap (Thule) index, F10.7 index, and Rz (sunspot number).
Also available at 1-day and 27-day resolution.
