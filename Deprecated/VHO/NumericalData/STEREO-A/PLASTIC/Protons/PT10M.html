STEREO-A PLASTIC 10-min Proton Plasma Parameters

ResourceID
spase://VHO/NumericalData/STEREO-A/PLASTIC/Protons/PT10M

Description

This data product contains 10-min averaged values of solar wind
proton flow speed, density, and temperature and corresponding thermal speed.
The records also hold flow velocity vector components and direction angles
in HERTN and RTN coordinates. Chi-squared and statistical uncertainties are
included, as are various flags. STEREO position information is given in radial
distance and HEE, HEEQ and HCI direction angles.
