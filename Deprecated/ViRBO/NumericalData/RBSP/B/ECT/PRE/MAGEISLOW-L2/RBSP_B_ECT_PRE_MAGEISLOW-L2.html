Omnidirectional Differential Electron Fluxes ~20-250 keV

Caveats
Omnidirectional Differential Electron Flux (FEDO). These data are obtained by averaging the sectored fluxes over the data accumulation time (a spin-set, nominally equal to 2 spins). Thus, it is not a true omni-directional flux, but rather a spin-set-averaged flux. Dimension 1 is for the 9 magEIS energy channels. The energy channel centroids are specified in FEDO_Energy. The channel widths are specified in FEDO_Energy_Widths.
